UBC OKANAGAN SUO CHINESE STUDENT & SCHOLAR ASSOCIATION

不列颠哥伦比亚大学奥肯纳根中国学生学者联谊会 (UBCO SUO CSSA), 成立于2018年，是不列颠哥伦比亚大学奥肯纳根校区(UBCO)学联会SUO及中国驻温哥华领事馆认证的奥肯纳根地区专属于中国学生学者的非盈利性组织。CSSA秉持着为广大中国学生学者筑就更优质的学习与生活环境，让远方亦如故乡般温暖的理念，为UBCO以及奥肯纳根地区成百上千名中国学生学者提供各类服务。

The University of British Columbia Okanagan Chinese Student and Scholar Association (UBCO SUO CSSA) was established in 2018. It is a non-profit organization for Chinese students and scholars in the Okanagan region, certified by the University of British Columbia Okanagan Coordination (UBCO) Student Union SUO and the Chinese Consulate in Vancouver. CSSA adheres to the concept of building a better learning and living environment for Chinese students and scholars, making the distant place as warm as home, and provides public services to hundreds of Chinese students and scholars in UBCO and the Okanagan region.
